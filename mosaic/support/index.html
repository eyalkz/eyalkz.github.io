Common questions

How do I back up my habits?
Settings → Import and Export → Export Backup (JSON) or Export CSV.

How do I restore a backup?
Settings → Import and Export → Import Data. Note: importing replaces the data currently on your device.

How do reminders work?
Add reminders per habit in the editor, or enable a single Daily Nudge in Settings → Reminders. Reminders are local to your device.

What does Mosaic Pro include?
Unlimited habits, the local AI coach, advanced insights, and import/export.

How do I cancel a subscription?
Manage or cancel anytime in your Apple Account settings (Settings app → your name → Subscriptions).

Is my data private?
Yes — see our Privacy Policy. Mosaic collects nothing; your data stays on your device (and your own iCloud if enabled).
