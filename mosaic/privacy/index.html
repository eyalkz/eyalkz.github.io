Mosaic — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Mosaic is a private, local-first habit tracker. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

The short version

Mosaic does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking. Your habit data lives on your device.

What data Mosaic stores

Habits you create — their names, descriptions, icons, colors, categories, schedules, reminders, completion history, and any notes or moods you add — are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. A display name you optionally enter is stored on-device only.

iCloud

If you enable iCloud sync (when available), your habit data is stored in your own private iCloud database and synced across your devices by Apple. Mosaic's developer cannot access this data. Apple processes it under Apple's Privacy Policy.

Notifications

Reminders are scheduled locally on your device. Mosaic does not send push notifications from a server and does not know whether or when a notification is delivered.

Purchases

Mosaic Pro is sold through Apple's In-App Purchase system. Apple processes the transaction; Mosaic receives only a purchase/entitlement status from Apple and never sees your payment details.

Data you export

You can export your data as a JSON backup or CSV file. These files are created by you, saved wherever you choose, and are never transmitted to the developer.

Children

Mosaic does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
